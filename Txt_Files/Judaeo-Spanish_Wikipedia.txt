Judaeo-Spanish (Ladino) Wikipedia Type of site Internet encyclopedia project Available in Judaeo-Spanish (Ladino) Owner Wikimedia Foundation URL lad.wikipedia.org Commercial No Registration Optional Judaeo-Spanish Wikipedia or Ladino Wikipedia is the Judaeo-Spanish-language version of Wikipedia. It was approved on December 21, 2006,[1] but its earliest articles date to August 2006.[2] Contents 1 Current status 2 Point of view 3 See also 4 References 5 External links Current status[edit] As of May 7, 2019 the Judaeo-Spanish Wikipedia had 3,856 articles. There were 15,484 registered users (including bots); 38 were active at that time, including 4 administrators.[3] Although Judaeo-Spanish was historically written in Hebrew script, today it is often written in Latin script. Judaeo-Spanish Wikipedia has content in both scripts, with the great majority of content in Latin script. This Latin script content reflects several different orthographic conventions that are variously used for Judaeo-Spanish. Point of view[edit] Judeo-Spanish is the historical language of the Sephardi Jews, and the largest concentration of its speakers is in Israel. Accordingly, much of the content of this encyclopedia—especially content not found in other Wikipedia projects—focuses on Sephardi Jewish culture and personalities, and on places and institutions of Israel.
