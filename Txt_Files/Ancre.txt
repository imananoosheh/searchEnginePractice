English Channel The Ancre (pronounced [??k?]; Picard: Inke) is a river of Picardy, France. Rising at Miraumont, a hamlet near the town of Albert, it flows into the Somme at Corbie. It crosses no départements other than the Somme. See also[edit] Battle of the Ancre Heights (October 1916) Battle of the Ancre (November 1916) Wikimedia Commons has media related to Ancre River.
