Marathi Wikipedia - Wikipedia Marathi Wikipedia From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Marathi Wikipedia Screenshot Screenshot of the Marathi Wikipedia home page Type of site Internet encyclopedia project Available in Marathi Owner Wikimedia Foundation URL mr.wikipedia.org Commercial No Registration Optional Launched 1 May 2003; 16 years ago (2003-05-01) The Marathi Wikipedia (Marathi: ????? ??????????) is the Marathi language edition of Wikipedia, a free and publicly editable online encyclopedia, and was launched on 1 May 2003. The project is one of the leading Wikipedia among other South Asian language Wikipedia's in various quality matrices.[1] It has grown on to become a Wiki containing more than 51,326 articles and has more than 88,175 registered users.[2] Among the most visited Marathi language websites, Marathi Wikipedia is ranked tenth by Alexa.[3] Contents 1 History 1.1 Beginning 1.2 Initial growth phase 2 Users and editors 2.1 Community and events 2.2 Media coverage and increased growth 3 Fonts and input methods 4 See also 5 References 6 External links History[edit] Beginning[edit] Marathi language Wikipedia is available in the wikipedia.org domain from 2003 May 1. 'Vasant Panchami'(???? ?????)[4] (Vasant Panchami) and 'Audumbar' (?????? (?????)), a poem by the poet Balkavi[5] were the first articles created on Marathi Wikipedia on 2 May 2003. Initial growth phase[edit] Marathi language Wikipedia picked up growth from 2006 onwards. On 13 January 2006 Marathi Wikipedia had 1500 articles. On 27 February Marathi Day is celebrated. Any one of Unicode Marathi fonts input system is fine for Marathi Wikipedia. Some people use inscript. Majority uses either Google phonetic transliteration or input facility Universal Language Selector provided on Marathi Wikipedia. Phonetic facility provided initially was java-based later supported by Narayam extension for phonetic input facility.
