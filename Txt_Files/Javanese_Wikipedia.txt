Javanese Wikipedia - Wikipedia Javanese Wikipedia From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Javanese Wikipedia Type of site Internet encyclopedia project Available in Javanese Headquarters Miami, Florida Owner Wikimedia Foundation URL jv.wikipedia.org Commercial No Registration Optional Javanese Wikipedia (Javanese: Wikipedia basa Jawa) is the edition of Wikipedia in the Javanese language. Started on 8 March 2004, the Javanese Wikipedia reached 10,000 articles on 3 May 2007. As of December 27, 2014, it has more than 48,000 articles.[1] The Indonesian media has discussed the Javanese Wikipedia.[2] Although the Wikipedia logo was written in the Javanese script since the beginning of the edition, the articles themselves could only be written in the Roman script until 2013.[3] Contents 1 Milestones 2 See also 3 References 4 External links Milestones[edit] Date Number Article name By User[4] Time[5] Article/day[6] 3 August 2010 30.000 Daun, Sangkapura, Gresik BlackKnight 7.123 52 day 19,23 July 13, 2009 20.000 Kandhangserang, Kandhangserang, Pekalongan Pras 3.858 day February 23, 2009 17.000 Paku-pakuan TracySurya 2.793 19 day 52,6 February 2, 2009 16.000 Abad kaping-3 Pras 2.648 79 day 12,7 November 15, 2008 15.000 Kabupatèn Kutai Kartanegara Pras 2.102 73 day 13,7 September 3, 2008 14.000 Padasaka, Candhimulya, Magelang Pras 1.673 98 day 10,2 May 28, 2008 13.000 Triskaidekafobia Meursault2004 1.053 118 day 8,47 February 10, 2008 12.000 Bucciano Pras 803 92 day 10,87 November 10, 2007 11.000 Pu?pacalita Meursault2004 718 191 day 5,24 3 May 2007 10.000 Margatiga, Lampung Timur Slamet Serayu 507 10 day 100 April 23, 2007 9.000 Tomohon Timur, Tomohon Slamet Serayu 499 18 day 55,6 April 5, 2007 8.000 Sugriwa Anggoro 485 2 day 500 April 3, 2007 7.000 224 PancenaBot 484 29 day 34,48 February 3, 2007 6.000 1856 Meursault2004 427 19 day xx January 15, 2007 5.000 Przemy?l Meursault2004 404 xx day xx July 25, 2006 2.000 Sarpanaka Anggoro 273 xx day xx May 7, 2005 1.000 Kakawin Ramayana Meursault2004 7 day 71,43 April 30, 2005 500 Keju Meursault2004 xx day xx July 29, 2004 200 Poerbatjaraka Meursault2004 20 day 5 July 9, 2004 100 Adigang, adigung, adiguna Meursault2004 123 day 0,81 March 8, 2004 1 Basa Jawa Meursault2004 3 0 day See also[edit] Papat Limpad, writing competition.
