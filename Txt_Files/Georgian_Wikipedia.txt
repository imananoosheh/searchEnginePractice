In 2019 the Georgian Wikipedia has 65 thousand unique categories and 23.55% of them do not have appropriate page in the category namespace. The average article in this language version has 3 categories, while number of unique categories per articles ratio is 0.509. The largest number of articles has Geography (24%) and History (14%) category. In Georgian Wikipedia articles related to Music and Organizations has the highest average quality.
