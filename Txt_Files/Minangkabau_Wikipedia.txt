Minangkabau Wikipedia - Wikipedia Minangkabau Wikipedia From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Minang Wikipedia Screenshot Mainpage of the Minang Wikipedia Type of site Internet encyclopedia project Available in Minang Headquarters Miami, Florida Owner Wikimedia Foundation URL min.wikipedia.org Commercial No Registration Optional Minang Wikipedia or Minangkabau Wikipedia (Minangkabau: Wikipedia baso Minang) is the edition of Wikipedia in the Minang language. Minang Wikipedia was approved by language committee on 29 January 2013, and the site was officially launched on 7 February. It reached 150,000 articles seven months later (9 September).[1] There are 222.623 articles in the Minang Wikipedia.
