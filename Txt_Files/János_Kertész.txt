János Kertész - Wikipedia János Kertész From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search János Kertész is a Hungarian physicist. He is one of the pioneers of econophysics, complex networks and application of fractal geometry in physical problems. He is the director of the Institute of Physics in Budapest University of Technology and Economics, Budapest, Hungary.[1] External links[edit] Personal Homepage References[edit] ^ "Archived copy".
