Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia - Wikipedia Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia Type of site Internet encyclopedia project Available in Nepal Bhasa Headquarters Miami, Florida Owner Wikimedia Foundation URL new.wikipedia.org Commercial No Registration Optional The Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia, is the Newar-language version of Wikipedia, run by the Wikimedia Foundation. Nepal Bhasa Wikipedia has currently 70,000 articles.[1] Nepal bhasa is the mother tongue language of Newars of Nepal. Newars are culturally rich ethnic group in Nepal.
