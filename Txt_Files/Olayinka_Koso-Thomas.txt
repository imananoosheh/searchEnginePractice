Olayinka Koso-Thomas - Wikipedia Olayinka Koso-Thomas From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Olayinka Koso-Thomas (born 1937) is a Nigerian-born doctor who lives in Sierra Leone. She is known internationally for her efforts to abolish female genital cutting.
