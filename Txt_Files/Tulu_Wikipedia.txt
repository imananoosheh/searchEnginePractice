Users 200 Launched August 2016 (as full site) Content license Creative Commons Attribution/ Share-Alike 3.0 (most text also dual-licensed under GFDL) Media licensing varies The Tulu Wikipedia is the Tulu language edition of Wikipedia, run by the Wikimedia Foundation.[1] It currently has over 1,000 articles. It is the 23rd language of India to get a Wikipedia after eight years in incubation.[2][3] Contents 1 History 2 Users and editors 3 See also 4 References 5 External links History[edit] Katherine Maher, the executive director of Wikimedia Foundation announced the launch of Tulu Wikipedia as a full site at the WIkiConference 2016.[1] It was in incubation since 2008.
