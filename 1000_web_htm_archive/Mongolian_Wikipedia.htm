Mongolian Wikipedia (Mongolian: Монгол Википедиа) – Wikipedia in Mongolian – is a free and publicly editable online encyclopedia. It was created on 28 February 2004. The Mongolian Wikipedia contains 18,969 articles. The number of active participants is relatively small, about 97, while the number of registered users is 61,474.

Growth, coverage and popularity[edit]

The growth of the Mongolian Wikipedia has been quite intense:

  • 17 November 2011 – 6,900 articles
  • 29 May 2012 – 7,593 articles
  • 29 May 2012 – 273,497 edits[1]

Language and dialects[edit]

Separate Wikipedias have been created for several Mongolian dialects, including Buryat (bxr:) and Kalmyk (xal:).
