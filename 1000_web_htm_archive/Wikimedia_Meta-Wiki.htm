Meta-Wiki or Wikimedia Meta-Wiki in its full name, often simply called “Meta”, is a wiki, dedicated to serving all other Wikimedia wikis in terms of policy discussions, debates, cross-wiki antivandalism, steward elections, and so on.[3][4]
