The Chavacano Wikipedia is an edition of the free online encyclopedia Wikipedia in the Chavacano language. As of March 27, 2020, this Wikipedia has 3,000 articles, 11,379 registered users, and 2 administrators,[1] making it the second smallest Philippine-based language version of Wikipedia.
