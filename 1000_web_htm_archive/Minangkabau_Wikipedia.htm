Minang Wikipedia or Minangkabau Wikipedia (Minangkabau: Wikipedia baso Minang) is the edition of Wikipedia in the Minang language. Minang Wikipedia was approved by language committee on 29 January 2013, and the site was officially launched on 7 February. It reached 150,000 articles seven months later (9 September).[1] There are 222.623 articles in the Minang Wikipedia.

Community room[edit]

Minang Wikipediawan community has community space in Kalawi, Padang.[2]
