The Occitan Wikipedia (Occitan: wikipèdia en occitan) is the Occitan language version of Wikipedia. The Occitan version of Wikipedia had 85,475 articles on 18 October 2013 (ranked 52nd among the 287 languages versions of Wikipedia).
