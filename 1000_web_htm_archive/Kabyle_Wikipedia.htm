Kabyle edition of Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The Kabyle Wikipedia is the Kabyle language edition of Wikipedia. Launched in April 2007,[1] It currently has 4,302 articles, making it the 209th largest Wikipedia by article count. This Wikipedia has 3 administrators along with 13,671 registered users and 25 active users.[2]
