Sindhi Wikipedia (Sindhi: سنڌي وڪيپيڊيا‎) is a free encyclopedia, started February 6, 2006. It is the Sindhi language edition of Wikipedia, a free, open-content encyclopedia. It has 12,475 articles.[1][2] Since 2014, the encyclopedia has experienced an overall increase in content.[3]

2006 to 2008[edit]

Wikipedia edition in Sindhi language is started in February 10, 2006 as a Sindhi Wikipedia by professor Ahsan Ahmed Aursani.[citation needed]

2015 to 2017[edit]

During this time Sindhi Wikipedia has expanded and some technical issues have been resolved. Sindhi Wikipedia users have improved content, references, technical issues, categorization, templates, and infoboxes. Recently Sindhi Wikipedians arranged a Wikipedia awareness workshop in Karachi.[4][5][6]
